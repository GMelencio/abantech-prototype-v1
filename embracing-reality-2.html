Defense & Security

The defense industry was among the first to adopt 3D technology initially as a training tool, simulating dangerous situations that require split second judgments. With Efficio in the mix, simulation transforms into situational awareness.

Efficio allows raw spatial data from multiple endpoints to be captured and fused into a real-time 3D model. Data from disparate systems are combined in a singular 3D display, giving a command center a real-time view of field operations. Plotting data points based on heat signatures, GPS coordinates and facial recognition systems, command decisions can be based on comprehensive awareness.

Efficio is device-agnostic – no more limitations between hardware, devices or applications. No complicated system integration, add endpoints as needed to build the 3D visualization needed to fulfill the mission.
